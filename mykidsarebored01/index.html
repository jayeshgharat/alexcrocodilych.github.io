Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is it really free?

    Yup. This service started as a way to occupy our own children. We figured it'd be valuable others.

  • Are you really going to refund me MORE than my paid subscription?

    Yup. We want to make sure your kids are not bored. If they're not enjoying the full list of activities, we'd like to make it easy to cancel and thank you for giving us a try.

  • Who curates and screens the activities and projects?

    We do...by hand. Yeah it's not quick but it's worth it.

  • Where do the activities come from?

    We have a range of sources. Some ideas are simplified versions found from the Internet while others are written for us by early childhood educators. All activities have sighted sources and credits.
