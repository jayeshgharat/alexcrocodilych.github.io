The Art of Growing an

Extraordinary Floral Business

It was time to wake up and smell the roses. I was 22 years old, I lived in Chicago, and I knew that I needed to find some sort of direction. I only had one problem... I didn’t really have a direction.

All I had were the things almost everyone else had…a family, some useless education, and some hobbies. None of my hobbies were anything I was particularly good at. But there was one thing I was extremely passionate about, something that I just inexplicably loved…

Flowers.

I loved them like children. I cared for them and created cute designs all the time to the point where it was maybe a little weird. But that’s kinda the thing isn’t it?

The things that we care about passionately, almost in an obsessive way, are usually the things that we could potentially make a business out of.

If you have that same passion for flowers that I do, then I have the solution for you.

Let’s Grow Together!

Growing Your Floral Business with Kelly Marie Thompson

Allow me to introduce my course, “The Art of Growing An Extraordinary Floral Business”. It’s your one-stop for learning how to turn your passion for flowers into money, just like I did

This course will walk you through all the steps you need, so you can make the right decisions for growth.

Your vision for your business can be your reality, and with encouragement, confidence, and inspiration, it’s my mission to work with you in order for you to craft a floral company that offers strategy and the profit margins you have been working for!

We’ll work on the beauty of business…together!
