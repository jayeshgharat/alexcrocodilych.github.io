Trading Contest

A trading contest is a war between all the traders to identify who will be the top-notch trader that gets the highest return. It is like a simulation where you use virtual money to trade, and not the real paper cash, although some contest uses paper cash. To win this contest, it takes different game plan and techniques that some may require you to break the rules of trading.

The target of the contest is to effectuate the inflated return within a certain period of time and not the one with the greatest return relative to risk. For you to get the triple digit in return, you have to risk your 5-50% of your account on every trade. If you are afraid to go big, then you should go home. This trading contest is not suitable for people with weak personality when it comes to risking money. It is like gambling, it either you play it, or you lose it. Getting on the top is your goal, although they have consolation prizes on the next rank, focus on your main goal in getting the first rank. It will test your strategies, boost your confidence and practice your skills. You can join this even if you have no experience trading, as long as you are ready for challenges, you are ready to join. Of course, before you involve yourself with this, especially if you are a beginner it is important to learn even the basic strategies of the trading contest.

You can start on the smaller one, and when you know that you are ready to join the bigger contest, then unleashed your hidden skills and start trading. It is too risky since it involves real money, but when you hit the top spot, it is totally worth it. It is kind of enjoying since it is like a game, you have to compete before you win. Just focus on your goal to be the number one, do not look at the second, third, be the best because the best will always be the number one and not number two.
