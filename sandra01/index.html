About PMU by Sandra

Permanent eyebrows can help you frame your face, highlight and open your eyes.

Beautiful brows create character to the face and reinforce your expression.

Our brows are the frame of the face and can significantly change your facial expression.

Permanent eyeliner is for most people the most scary treatment to get done.

However, I would like to point out that it in no way hurts or is dangerous.

Permanent eyeliner on large or small eyes can be pigmented so that your face visually looks more in balance and your eyes are highlighted best.

We are fully aware of the importance of eyebrows for most women, and at PMU by Sandra our goal is to highlight your natural beauty.
