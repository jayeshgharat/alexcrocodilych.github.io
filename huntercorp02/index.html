Work with the best

At Hunter Corp, the process of designing and building your home will begin with a detailed concept and plan. We will meet with you as often as needed in order to develop the perfect draft.

Our inhouse architect and CAD expert will create a plan that fits your unique ideas and lifestyle.

Other experts at our organization include Roger Hunter, our president, who has been designing and building luxury homes for more than 35 years.

Once you are satisfied with the plan, our team will begin construction on your dream home. By drawing from, and combining the strengths of our professions, we deliver a product unparalleled in the industry.
