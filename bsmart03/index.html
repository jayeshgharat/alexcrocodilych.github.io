About "B SMART"

"B SMART" specialises in earthworks, landscaping, drainage, driveway construction and all types of rural services. With extensive experience in the hills we can offer genuine advice on materials and the best way to execute your project. Delivering projects and living in the area means our local experience will give you a solution that actually works and will last.

Quality and customer service are very important in a small community as we seek return customers and word of mouth referrals. Therefore we are focused on making sure our customers get what they ask for and are 100% happy with the finished product. What separates "B SMART" from the competitors is we guarantee our work. We also offer our free planning and guidance from conception through to completion.

We are mad about recycling and can recommend good recycled and up-cycled products for your property while reusing your existing materials where possible. Our philosophy is to work with the land and nature and leave a light footprint on the landscape.
