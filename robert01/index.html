The firm

Who we are

Robert Reid Business Brokers specializes in the sale of small and lower mid-market privately-held and family-owned companies in the Mid-Atlantic Region, Virginia, Maryland, and Washington DC areas.

Robert Reid Business Brokers will consult with you to understand the value of your business and the goals you would like to achieve. We will provide aggressive, confidential marketing and increase the pool of potential buyers.

We will screen and qualify serious buyers with real interest. Once a good match has been identified for your business, we will help you negotiate the terms and structure to achieve the best possible outcome for you.
