Simplify Your Recruiting Process

The connection between data from 2 million school list and your personal taste profile is made by right-school.com algorithms. This is the secret sauce, and it gets complicated quickly.

Our approaches include collaborative filtering, most commonly seen in Amazon's "customers who bought this item also bought…" feature, and natural language processing, which is how it understands school blogs and the titles of school lists. We uses the open-source software Kafka to manage the data in real-time.
