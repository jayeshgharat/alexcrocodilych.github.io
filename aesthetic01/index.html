Which Service Could Work For You?

  • Chemical Skin Peels

    As one of the top 10 most commonly performed aesthetic procedures, clinic-based skin or chemical peels complement a good skincare regimen. They can also enhance the effects of injectable treatments. They work by removing dead skin cells and stimulating the growth of new cells, promoting a more youthful and radiant appearance.

  • Dermal & Lip Fillers

    Dermal fillers are injected under the skin to rejuvinate these areas by replacing the lost volume. These fillers are gels that are made from Hyaluronic Acid, which naturally occurs in our bodies. They can also contain a local anaesthetic to make the treatment more comfortablebr for you.

  • Anti-Wrinkle Injections

    Throughout our lives, we laugh, frown and raise our brows. The consistent movement of the facial muscles result in lines, some of which can become more permanent. Anti-wrinkle treatment using Botulinum toxin can precisely target these lines by relaxing the muscles. The treatment is quick and virtually pain free with no downtime required.

We Are... Iki & Dr Chris

We are Iki and Dr Chris, a Brighton-based wife and husband team with over 25 years of experience in healthcare between us.
