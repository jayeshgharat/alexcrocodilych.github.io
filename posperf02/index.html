Positive coaching

Coaching is a dialog between two equal partners, a coach and a client (or coachee). The relationship between the two is built on trust. Usually, the initial coaching conversation, also called a discovery session, reveals the level of trust between a potential client and the coach.

No amount of coach’s experience or references can replace client’s intuition when it comes to trust. It is common for coaches to be trusted with information that are very personal in nature. Once the relationship is established a more experienced coach will be more effective in supporting the client.

Coach is responsible for creating a safe space where a client can come with confidence and trust to share the matters freely. This space is free of coach’s agenda or judgment, yet full of respect, support, and positive energy.
