The company

Who we are

Since its organization in 1980, Sotomayor Consulting has provided corporate tax consulting and legal advisory to the most important domestic and foreign companies, covering various areas of the economic activity.

Our mission is still to provide top-quality tax and legal services, under independence and integrity standards. Our team is composed of lawyers, auditors, and commercial engineers.

On this "multidisciplinary" platform we work as a team, gathering various points of view and providing customized integral tax and legal solutions. We appreciate the opportunity to assist you in the discussion and solution of the tax and legal needs of your business.
