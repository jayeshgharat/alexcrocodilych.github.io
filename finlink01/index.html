About us

Core values

  • Honestly and Integrity

    To always be fair, to ensure the clients best interested is always considered, and to conduct ourselves with integrity.

  • Safety

    Safety is everyone responsibility. No one deserves to get injured for wanting to complete a project, it is our goal that we keep a our sites with zero incidents at all times.

  • Culture

    To encourage and support our employees to expand their goals and skills. When a team knows the management is grateful and respects them, it ensures projects will be completed with highest standards and on budget and time.

  • Performance

    Perform and complete every job, on time, and on budget. To ensure the end client/user is left completely satisfied.

  • Environment

    We encourage all our staff, and trade contractors to reuse, recycle, and salvage whereever and whenever possible.

Our projects

We build it!

Finlink Construction Ltd was incepted in 2012, prior to that the owners of the company worked for BC's most reputable 'medium-size' commercial construction companies.

Our projects are a mix of new construction, and renovation work, ranging from $1,000 to $5,000,000.

At Finlink Construction Ltd, construction is our passion, we enter every project with our company philosophy of "Always in the best interest of the project" in mind.

Our goal is not only to complete every project on time and budget, but as well as to provide a superior product.
