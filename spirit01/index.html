At Spirit Cars, we're passionate about the things we build and the people we build them for. Spirit Cars offers a wide array of fiberglass bodies, chassis, parts and pieces to build your car to completion.

IWe have been in business making dreams come true since 1994. We offer a broad selection kits for 1923 T-Buckets, 1927 T-Buckets, 1923 C-Cabs, '28-29 and '30'31 Model A Coupe, Sedan, and Sedan Delivery.

We are also pleased to announce that we have kits available for 32 3 and 5-Window Ford Coupes along with a 34 3 window Ford Coupe. We offer every variation from bolt kits to finished cars.

We realize and thank the many customers that we now are able to call friends and part of the Spirit family that have made our dreams become reality building theirs.
