Welcome to Woodend Produce Store

For over 20 years we at "Woodend Produce Store" have provided the finest in farm, feed and pet supplies to the Woodend area. Open 7 days a week, our friendly and knowledgeable staff is here to help you with all of your animal needs.

We pride ourselves on carrying the best in quality food, an interesting variety of toys, shampoos, collars, leashes and other supplies and treats to help keep your dog or cat healthy and happy. We have the best hay, grain and feed for your horses and farm animals as well as fencing, gates, panels, feeding containers, etc. Plus with free pet food delivery to the Woodend area, and delivery to most area stables and barns twice a week, we can help you have more time to enjoy your four legged creature. We also have a great selection of bird and other small animal feeds, treats and toys.

And if we don't carry it, we welcome an opportunity to fill your special orders.
