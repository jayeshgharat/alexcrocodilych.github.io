About us

Constructed for life!

Specializing in rustic home decor, our creative team gathers inspiration from sourcing existing pieces and repurposing them. Whether it’s a decorative ladder, bud vase or decorative planter, every piece in our store has been handcrafted by us. We take great pride in our designs and build each item with careful attention to every detail.

Our handmade furniture and wooden home decor is more than just another item in your home. Every piece of unique furniture is created with an attention to detail that is unmatched.

Our unique furniture line and rustic home decor such as bud vases, rustic planters and decorative ladders is guaranteed to add a bit of style and charm to your home. Our handmade, rustic wooden furniture is the perfect addition to any home.

From accent pieces to large furniture, we are happy to create custom pieces just for you. All you need to do is let us know what you are looking for and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

Inspared? Naturally.

We Offer the following wood & finish options. It is important to note that all stain options are limited to the wood category that they are shown on below. Also refer to actual real wood samples to make your selection as images can vary from wood samples.

  • Brushwork Maple

    During the preparation of materials we use a special acclimatizing pre-treatment procedure. We then meticulously add our unique hand applied “BRUSHED” distressing technique. The end result is a distinctly modern-rustic appearance reminiscent of warm windswept prairie lands.

  • Buxton Cherry

    Our Buxton Cherry begins with premium solid Black Cherry, widely considered one of the finest materials for building handcrafted furniture. The natural beauty of Black Cherry wood comes from the unique markings and colour variations present in every harvested tree.

  • Heritage Maple

    Our Heritage Maple comes from the same trees as our standard Maple. The only difference is that this Maple was also once home to the Ambrosia Beetle. Insects and soil conditions affect the appearance of every tree.

Mission
