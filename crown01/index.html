Support & EMPOWERMENT

Crown of Texas Hospice Foundation was started in June 1991 to support the unique services of Hospice programs.

Your gift to the Crown of Texas Hospice Foundation provides for the special needs of terminally ill patients and their families and is tax deductible.

Activities the foundation may cover include various forms of financial assistance for patients and families, bereavement support for patient's families as well as the community, volunteer activities, and educational activities.

Financial assistance for patients and families may include special needs that are generally not covered under the Hospice program.

Examples include, but are not limited to, items for quality living such as clothes, rent, telephone, food, caregiver support, dental care, medications, durable medical equipment, and transportation.
