Work with the best

BMI Real Estates has been working with American military and civilian clients (many with high ranks and GS-ratings) for over 20 years, and have always reflected a professional and high-standard of expertise in the area of rental properties… providing the best service possible to all of our clients.

We pride ourselves in finding the right home-away-from-home, because we know how important it is to feel good about the place we live.

Our goal is to ensure that each and every one of our clients achieve the desired result, whether it’s wealth preservation, liquidity, finding a “best value” lease alternative or income generation.

We have helped hundreds of people stationed here find a place to live that suits their lifestyles best.

We look forward working with you.
